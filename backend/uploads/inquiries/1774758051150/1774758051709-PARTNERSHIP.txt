BACKGROUND: IDB Global, as Party No. 1, specializes in providing tailored 
solutions to students aspiring to study in Finland. This agreement outlines 
the terms of collaboration between the Parties for the recruitment of 
students for undergraduate, graduate studies, and language courses in 
Finland. 
TERMS AND CONDITIONS: 
A. GENERAL & SERVICE CHARGES: 
1. Commission: Party No. 1 will pay commissions Party No. 2 to enroll 
candidates from Finland for studies and/or language courses. 
2. Recruitment: Party No. 2 agrees to recruit students for universities 
represented by Party No. 1. 
3. Information: Party No. 1 will provide comprehensive information on 
universities, courses, and the application process.

4. Consultation Charges: Charges for student consultations must adhere to Party 
No. 1's pricing; non-compliance may lead to the agreement's termination. 
5. Service Fee: Party No. 2 agrees to charge a service fee at least equal to that of 
Party No. 1 to maintain market exclusivity. 
6. Commission Payment: Party No. 1 will be liable to pay Party No. 2 a commission as 
per the individual institution, subject to written approval from Party No. 1. The 
commission is valid only for the 1st year. 
B. STUDENT, TRAINING & MARKETING SERVICES: 
1. Training: Party No. 1 will provide training to Party No. 2 for student counseling, 
either at Party No. 1's office in Kathmandu or through an online session, as 
required. 
2. Responsibilities: Party No. 2 is responsible for preparing students for entrance 
exams and visa interviews. Party No. 2 will bear the full cost of marketing Party 
No. 1's services. 
3. Tuition Fee Payment: Party No. 2 is solely responsible for ensuring students pay 
their tuition fees on time after receiving the offer letter. 
C. FEE PAYMENTS AND OFFER LETTERS: 
1. Documentation: Party No. 2 is entitled to complete health insurance and 
arrange all required documents. 
D. ACCOMMODATION & POST-VISA SERVICES: 
1. Accommodation: Party No. 1 will provide Party No. 2 with the accommodation 
link and guidance on how to register and apply for student housing. Party No. 
2 will be responsible for guiding and assisting their students throughout the 
accommodation application process. 
2. Assistance: If Party No. 2 requires assistance with student transfers from 
Helsinki to the final destination, Party No. 1 may provide help at the written 
request of Party No. 2 (subject to availability). 
E. REFUND & DEFERMENT POLICIES: 
1. Refund Policies: During the joint and rolling application period, refund policies 
will align with universities' website information.
